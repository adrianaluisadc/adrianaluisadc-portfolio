About Me 

Adriana Luisa Dela Cruz

 General Santos City, Philippines

Mabuhay! I am a self-taught UX designer from the Republic of the Philippines. A versatile individual and a constant learner. Always been passionate about design, philosophical thought, and critical thinking. You can check out my resume here.

Philosophies

  • Believes in the power of learning in every circumstances. May it be good or worse, something is always there for me to grab and discover.
  • Humanity matters always. Growing up, I have discovered that I am empathetic by nature. I feel human’s emotions and sufferings as if I am on their shoes. I find this part of me very helpful especially when I think about the users of the products during the design process.
  • Every success begins with a single step. Process—may it be with life or design—is tedious and requires every effort. Albeit you might get lost in the middle of the journey or get burned out, it is important to trust it. Going hastily will breed greater destruction, and going slowly would slip you out of opportunities. Just trust the flow and do the best of what you can now.
